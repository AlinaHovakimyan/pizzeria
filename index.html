About Our Pizzeria

Welcome to Schwenksaal Pizza, where authentic Italian tradition meets modern culinary excellence. Our pizzeria has been serving the Bayreuth community with passion and dedication, crafting each pizza with the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques.


From our wood-fired oven to our hand-stretched dough, every element of our pizza-making process is designed to deliver an authentic Italian experience. We believe that great pizza starts with great ingredients, which is why we source only the finest mozzarella, San Marzano tomatoes, and fresh herbs.


Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch, a romantic dinner, or a family celebration, we're committed to providing you with an unforgettable dining experience that captures the true spirit of Italian hospitality.
